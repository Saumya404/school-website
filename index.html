SAFETY & SECURITY

Excellent Safety & Security

Safety and Security is the prime focus at Madhusudhan Global School. It is the key responsibility of our school to ensure the safe & secure environment for students, staff and visitors. With the care and concern the management has come up with many initiatives. • Entire building is equipped with the fire fighting equipment. • Security Persons in both the gate to keep the continuous check on the arrival and exit of visitors and students. • CCTV's are installed in all the class rooms and at the strategic locations like Corridors,staircase and gates to track the flow of traffic. • The school also have the first aid facility. Prompt attention is given in case of medical emergencies. • Hospital Facility is nearby like Deepak Hospital, Thunga Hospital. • Our children are always accompanied with lady attendant in buses.
